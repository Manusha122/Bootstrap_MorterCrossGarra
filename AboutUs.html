WELCOME TO MOTOCROSS GEAR

SMALL BEGINNINGS

Started in a small office above a motorcycle shop in the heart of Brighton, selling excess stock from our sister company, retail outlet ‘On The Wheel’. We quickly sold out of the limited parts we had, and it was clear there was a market for affordable motorcycle parts.
